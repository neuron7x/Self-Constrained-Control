# Code of Conduct

This project follows the **Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct**.

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, hate speech, threats, or doxxing
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without permission
- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking appropriate corrective action.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the maintainers (see `SECURITY.md` for preferred contact patterns). For safety, do **not** open a public issue for reports involving personal data.
